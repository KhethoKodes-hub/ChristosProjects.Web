About Us

Building Dreams, Securing Futures, Empowering Communities

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Christios Projects (Pty) Ltd emerged from a vision to address the growing demand for quality construction and security services in the Northern Cape Province. Based in Postmasburg within the Tsantsabane municipality, we have positioned ourselves as a cornerstone of development in our district.

Our company is registered with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission under registration number 2022/275150/07. What sets us apart is not just our comprehensive service offering, but our commitment to excellence and community empowerment.

From our inception, we have specialized in a diverse range of services including Security, Building Construction, Maintenance, Tiling, Roofing, Plumbing, Painting, Paving, Concrete work, Fencing, Steel Works, and Electrical Installations. This comprehensive approach allows us to be a one-stop solution for all our clients' needs.

Ms. Christine Santa Van Der Westhuizen

Founder & Director

The driving force behind Christios Projects, Ms. Christine Santa Van Der Westhuizen brings a wealth of experience and diverse skill set to the organization. With 16 years of distinguished service in the South African Police Service, she has developed an acute understanding of security, discipline, and operational excellence.

Her transition from law enforcement to entrepreneurship was driven by a passion to contribute to the economic development of her community while creating employment opportunities for local residents. Her leadership style emphasizes integrity, accountability, and a commitment to quality that permeates every aspect of our operations.

Under her guidance, Christios Projects has grown from a startup vision into a trusted name in construction and security services, with ambitious plans for expansion across multiple provinces in the coming years.
