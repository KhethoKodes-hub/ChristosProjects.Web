Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2022

CHRISTIOS PROJECTS is a registered proprietary limited company, registered with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) with registration number 2022/275150/07. Situated in Postmasburg (Tsantsabane) in the Northern Cape Province, our business was established to meet the growing demand for development in the district.

As a 100% Black Women-Owned enterprise with BEE Level 1 certification, we take pride in our contribution to Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment while delivering exceptional services across multiple sectors.

Our diverse portfolio spans Security Services, Construction, Building, Maintenance, Tiling, Roofing, Plumbing, Painting, Paving, Concrete work, Fencing, Renovations, Steel Works, and Electrical Installations and Repairs.
