About Christios Projects

Your Trusted Partner in Construction & Security

Established in 2022, Christios Projects (Pty) Ltd is a registered proprietary limited company based in Postmasburg, Northern Cape Province. We were founded to meet the growing demand for quality development in the district.

Led by Ms. Christine Santa Van Der Westhuizen, who brings 16 years of experience from the South African Police Service, we combine expertise, integrity, and local knowledge to deliver exceptional results.

Our mission is to provide reliable, excellent, and quality service to all clients in the Northern Cape Province while contributing to Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment (B-BBEE) and reducing unemployment.
