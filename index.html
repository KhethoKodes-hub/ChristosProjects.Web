About Christios Projects

Registered with CIPC (2022/275150/07), Christios Projects is a 100% Black Women-Owned company with BEE Level 1 certification, based in Postmasburg, Northern Cape Province. Established to meet the demand of development in the district, led by Ms. Christine Santa Van Der Westhuizen with 16 years of experience in the South African Police Service.

Our Vision

To become the foremost reliable company in the Northern Cape Province, providing security and maintenance services while contributing to Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment. To expand in the next five years and become one of the leading Black Owned Managed Companies in the Mining and Building Construction sectors.

Our Mission

To provide reliable service to all clients in the Northern Cape Province. We strive to deliver excellent and quality service, building sustainable partnerships with our clients and partners.

Our Goals

Deliver value-added services above competitor standards, engage in effective communication, build relationships with contractors and mining organizations, appoint qualified and experienced staff, expand to other provinces, and reinvest profits to grow into surrounding areas.
